About Us

Let me first introduce myself. My name is Brian Jennings and I've been a home inspector for 30+ years! I started out doing pest control with my father in California then we branched out and included home inspections. I later moved to Daingerfield, TX in 2014 and couldn't be happier! I started this business on my own and have been serving the East Texas community since.

Whether you are buying your first home, your 50th home, selling, or a realtor looking for a solid inspector, I'm your guy! I have many contacts and relationships throughout East Texas and am excited to make more! Buying or selling a home can be quite the event, and I'm here to make it as smooth and pain-free as possible.

Buying

Buying a home is one of the most delicate processes in the professional world. Therer are so many moving parts, forms to fill out and sign, and so on. The last thing you want to do is move in to your lovely new home, and BAM!, house is completely flooded or the A/C just doesn't work. That's what I'm here to prevent! I have a complete and thourough process which makes sure that the home you're buying isn't complete garbage! When you hire me, you're hiring confirmation that the home you just agreed 30-years to pay for is going to last.

Selling

You may not think you need an inspector when you sell your house, but the answer is, you should! Nothing can destory a home sale quick like an inspection going south when your days into escrow. Why not prevent all that mess by getting your home inspected before listing it! This way, when your soon to be buyers get it inspected, you've already fixed those pesky little issues that seem to pop up. This makes it look like your home is well taken care of and a great buy!
